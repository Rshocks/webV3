Running

Here are a few quick tips about running in Galway!

1- Shoes, shoes, shoes, running is very hard to get used too if your shoes are in poor quality so don't be afraid to invest.

2- Pace yourself for long distance running it's a marathon not a sprint most average roughly 12-14 minutes per mile.

3- Don't be afraid to fail and not reach your running goal it's not the end of the world, just keep trying one step at a time.
